# Support

Thanks for using FuseMeld! Here's how to get help depending on what you need.

---

## 🐛 Found a bug?

Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/Codewithpabitra/FuseMeld/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) — fill out the template so it's easy to reproduce and fix.

## 💡 Have a feature idea?

Open a [Feature Request](https://github.com/Codewithpabitra/FuseMeld/issues/new?template=feature_request.md) — describe the problem it solves and who it helps.

## ❓ General questions?

Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Codewithpabitra/FuseMeld/discussions) for anything that isn't a bug or feature — setup help, how something works, ideas, feedback.

## 📖 Read the docs first

- [README.md](README.md) — setup and usage
- [ARCHITECTURE.md](ARCHITECTURE.md) — system design and technical decisions
- [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) — how to contribute

Please do not open issues for general questions — they will be closed and redirected here.
